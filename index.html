ABOUT

G'day! Fellow Cycling Legends!

I am Fabien, your dedicated Mobile Bicycle Doctor (MBD)!

My mission is to provide safe and reliable repairs, ensuring that your beloved bicycle is in top-notch condition. Much like you, I have a passion for cycling and the thrill it brings.

I love the rush that comes from a great ride, the freedom afforded by two wheels, and the holistic benefits for my body and mind. There's nothing better than jumping on a trusty machine and cruising at full speed through the wilderness, vineyards, or along scenic cycle paths.

However, what I hate is when something goes wrong with my bicycle - a flat tyre, an unexpected squeak, loss of breaking, or equipment malfunction. In such moments of distress, that's when you contact me - your Mobile Bicycle Doctor always here to help! With two decades of experience under my belt, I've perfected my skills in repairing and maintaining bicycles of all types, be it children's bikes, adult mountain bikes, road bikes, hybrid bikes or even fixies.

My primary goal is to guarantee the safety, reliability, and roadworthiness of your bicycle. I'm here to assist you, your family and friends enjoy countless hours of adventure on two wheels.

And please, remember to Always Ride Safe!

Fabien - MBD

Bonjour! I'm Fabien, originally from France, and I've called the beautiful city of Adelaide home since 2018. Trained as a mechanical engineer, I spent four years in the automotive industry. But my heart has always been with bicycles, leading me to establish my very own mobile bicycle repair service here in Adelaide.

My love for cycling began when I was just a toddler. By the age of 4, I was confidently navigating on two wheels. But with every ride came a question: How long would it be before my trusty bicycle needed a tune-up? This curiosity sparked my two-decade-long journey into bicycle repair and maintenance, where I've worked on bikes of every conceivable style and size.
