MOBILE BICYCLE DOCTOR

ABOUT

G'day! Fellow Cycling Legends!

I'm Fabien and I'm your Mobile Bicycle Doctor (MBD)!

I'm here to provide safe and reliable repairs for your best bicycle! Like you, I have a passion for bikes and riding!

I love the rush that comes from a great ride, the independence that two wheels provide, and the benefits for my body and mind. There's nothing better than jumping on a trusty machine and riding at full speed across the bush, through the vineyard or along cycle paths.

What I hate is when something is wrong with my bike. A flat tyre, a sudden squeak, loss of breaking or equipment failure. That's when you contact me - your Mobile Bicycle Doctor always here to help! I have 20 years of experience repairing and maintaining bikes of all shapes, styles and sizes. From children's bikes, to adult mountain bikes, road bikes, and hybrid bikes and even fixies. I am also a qualified Mechanical and Industrial Engineer, with 4 years of experience working in the automotive industries.

I'm here to ensure that your bike is safe, reliable and roadworthy. I'm here to help you, your family and friends enjoy many hours of adventure on two wheels!

And please, remember to Always Ride Safe!

Fabien (Mobile Bicycle Doctor)

Fabien - MBD

My name is Fabien, I’m French, and have been living in Australia since 2018. I am a mechanical engineer by trade, and worked as an automotive engineer for 4 years, but I have finally decided to settle in Adelaide to start my own business as a mobile bicycle mechanic.

From what my parents told, I started my first cycling experience at 2 years old of age, and already cycling confidently at 4…. I was definitely made to ride! I was confident and having no fear, but the raising question: how long will this two-wheel machine last before any mechanical issues? That was the first step of my long learning journey as a bicycle mechanic! Now it is more than 20 years of repairs and maintenance on bicycles of all shapes, styles and sizes that I have undertaken.

In my vision, cycling means freedom and adrenaline, but also health and well-being. The after COVID situation has really impacted the way people think about mobility. Riding has always been considered for recreational activities, but nowadays it is more and more valued as the best alternative for transportation on a daily basis. Indeed, cars usage is becoming so costly, fuel is getting so expensive! So why not saving money daily by using your best bicycle?

On that note, Mobile Bicycle Doctor (MBD) will do everything to satisfy your needs, by ensuring that your bicycle is safe, reliable, roadworthy and serviced at an affordable cost. I am entirely mobile, which means more convenience and flexibility. If you are stuck on the road, or want to get rid of the stress and inconvenience of reaching the nearest bicycle store (transportation, time, booking issues….), that’s when you contact me: your Mobile Bicycle Doctor to help!
